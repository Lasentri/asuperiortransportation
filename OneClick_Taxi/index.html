FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need coding experience?
No. The 10-step setup wizard guides you through everything. If you can install a WordPress plugin and copy/paste an API key, you can set this up. For advanced customization, we recommend pairing with Claude Pro AI.
What WordPress version do I need?
WordPress 6.0 or higher, PHP 8.0 or higher. You also need an SSL certificate (HTTPS) on your site for Square payments to work.
Can I move it to a new server later?
Yes. Deactivate the plugin on your current server first — this releases the license. Then install on your new server and enter the same key. The license transfers cleanly.
What if the setup wizard doesn't work for me?
Email us at stalcollc@gmail.com. We'll help you get it running. If the issue is on our end, we'll make it right. See our No-Refund Policy above for details.
Do I need a Square account?
Yes for card payments, but it's free to sign up at squareup.com. If you don't want to accept cards, the booking system still works — customers can pay cash and the dispatcher handles payment separately.
What is Pushover and do I have to use it?
Pushover is a $5 one-time app that sends instant push notifications to your phone when a booking comes in. It's optional but highly recommended — without it you rely on email notifications which may be delayed.
Can I customize the colors and logo?
Yes. The setup wizard includes a 4-color brand picker and logo upload. Your booking page will match your company's look.
What happens when my license expires?
The plugin will display a renewal notice in your admin. Your booking page stays active for a grace period. Renew at any time from the License Department to continue receiving updates and support.
